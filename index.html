About this Dog

The Golden Retriever is a large-sized breed of dog bred as gun dogs to retrieve shot waterfowl such as ducks and upland game birds during hunting and shooting parties. Named 'retriever' because of their ability to retrieve shot game undamaged, Golden Retrievers have an instinctive love of water and are easy to train to basic or advanced obedience standards. They are one of the most popular family dogs in the world due to their friendly, reliable, and trustworthy personality.
